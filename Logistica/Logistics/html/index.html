about us

GRUPO LOGISTICA is a full service company specialized in importation, warehousing, distribution and merchandising of consumer products with a wide variety of specialized services. We provide world wide logistics that service the major retailers in Mexico. We bring any product, from everywhere in the world, to the stores in México and abroad on time and locating where strategically it is to be sold.

You can trust us to fulfill the needs of your trade in all of the distribution stages. We are committed to meet your logistical needs in every step of the way. We provide our partners with the benefits of a global strategic network and logistics. All of our operations are coordinated by qualified and experienced specialists.

Our service and attention are personalized; we analyze the specific needs of our associates, enhancing the efficiency of procedures and optimizing costs and deliveries, in order to meet the Bottom Line. Our priority is to form long term partnerships based on mutual goals.
